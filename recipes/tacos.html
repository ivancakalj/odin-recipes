Low Carb Tacos

  • Description

    • This is a great low-carb alternative to your standard
    homemade tacos. I love Mexican food and wasn't willing
    to part with tacos after starting my low-carb diet.
    This always satisfies my craving.

  • Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • ½ (4 ounce) can diced jalapeno peppers
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce

  • How to make

  1. Cook and stir ground beef,
    onion, and jalapeno peppers together in a skillet over
    medium-high heat until
    meat is browned and crumbly, 7 to 10 minutes.
  2. Stir taco seasoning into meat mixture;
    bring to a simmer and cook until flavors combine,
    about 5 minutes
